# The Game for APH

- Genre: Adventure
- Library: PixiJS
- Space: 2D world, non eclidean
- Objects: animated player, flashlight, portals, rooms, enemies
- Actions
    - walking, pointing flashlight, avoiding enemies
- Rules
    - the goal is to find and complete tasks and find the exit
    - the player have to avoid enemies at all cost
    - tasks are randomly placed
- Technical mechanic
    - locations, keys, doors, exploration, 
    - portals - one sided or two sided
    - enemy behavior - chasing the player
